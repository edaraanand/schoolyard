Community Alert — designed for schools to stay connected with their learning community via text and voice messages, whenever they require prompt, reliable and cost-effective method to deliver time sensitive notices. Community Alert offers a complete solution for executing and organizing text and voice messages for schools through a comprehensive web solution that does not require investment in expensive hardware or software.


Prompt Delivery

Using this product, messages are sent and received almost immediately; thus, ensuring well-timed information. Faster than email. One step process.

Track Acknowledgment

Keep track of your messages – Community Alert also helps to allow acknowledgment of message receivers, and the date & time when the message was received.

User-Friendly

With minimal training required, its simple user interface allows your school staff to start using the system from day one. Your staff can access the system from anywhere via an internet connection.

Variety Of Uses

You can use our Community Alert system in so many different ways. Given below are some of the situations in which schools are finding it useful for sending out time-sensitive alerts.

  • Emergency Alerts (e.g., lockdown)
  • Event notifications
  • Class cancellations
  • Ill children
  • Absentee students
  • School bus delays
  • Local dangers
  • Weather School Closure
  • Important school event reminder
  • Event rescheduling
